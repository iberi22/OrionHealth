# Privacy in the Age of AI

In an era where data is often called the new oil, medical information is perhaps the most precious and sensitive data an individual possesses. As Artificial Intelligence (AI) becomes increasingly integrated into healthcare, a critical question arises: Can we reap the benefits of AI without sacrificing our privacy?

## The Problem with Cloud-Based AI

Traditional AI models typically run in the cloud. When you interact with a cloud-based AI, your data—be it symptoms, lab results, or personal history—is transmitted to a server, processed, and often stored. This creates several risks:

1.  **Data Breaches:** Centralized databases are prime targets for hackers.
2.  **Corporate Misuse:** Data can be sold or used for targeted advertising.
3.  **Lack of Control:** Once your data is in the cloud, you lose ultimate control over it.

## The OrionHealth Solution: On-Device AI

At OrionHealth, we've taken a different approach. We believe that your health data is sacred. That's why we've built our assistant to run **entirely on your device**.

### How it Works

By leveraging powerful local databases like Isar and optimized AI models, OrionHealth processes your information right where it belongs: on your phone.

-   **Local Storage:** Your records never leave your device.
-   **Local Processing:** When you ask our AI about your health, the "thinking" happens locally. No data is sent to external servers for inference.
-   **Encryption at Rest:** Your data is protected with military-grade AES-256 encryption.

## Why it Matters

On-device AI isn't just a technical feat; it's a commitment to human rights. It ensures that:

-   **You are the sole owner of your data.**
-   **Your privacy is guaranteed by design, not just by a policy.**
-   **You can access intelligent health insights even without an internet connection.**
