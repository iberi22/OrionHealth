    <h1>Get Started with OrionHealth</h1>

    <h2>Installation</h2>
    <p>Currently, OrionHealth is available as an Android APK.</p>
    <ol>
        <li>Download the latest APK from the <a href="https://github.com/iberi22/local-llm.OrionHealth/releases">Releases page</a>.</li>
        <li>Enable "Install from Unknown Sources" on your Android device settings.</li>
        <li>Open the APK file to install.</li>
    </ol>

    <h2>Prerequisites</h2>
    <ul>
        <li>Android 10 or higher</li>
        <li>At least 4GB of RAM (recommended for AI features)</li>
        <li>200MB free storage space</li>
    </ul>

    <h2>Troubleshooting</h2>
    <p>If the app crashes on startup, ensure you have granted the necessary storage permissions.</p>
